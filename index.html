Game Controls


  • Player 1 Controls: Use W, A, S, D to move and press E to shoot.
  • Player 2 Controls: Use Arrow Keys to move and press / to shoot.

Game Rules


  • +30 points for hitting the opponent.
  • -10 points if you get hit by a bullet.
  • -1 point for every bullet shot.
  • The game lasts for 60 seconds. Score as high as you can!
